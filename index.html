Pictures tell more than words.

Remember that sunny day when you jumped in the blue ocean and suddenly found yourself swimming in the middle of a fish swarm? What the salt water on your skin felt like?
Or that one time when you drove 20 hours by bus, went out in the freezing cold and saw the breathtaking dance of the northern lights on the nightsky?
When you wanted to hike up that snowy volcano and saw it erupting from the parking lot already?

Capture your story the way you lived it.

It doesn't always have to be the most extreme story or the most extraordinary moment. Each one of us experiences wonderful moments and events worth capturing them every single day! What's crucial is that it's your story and holds meaning for you.

Photography isn't solely about grand spectacles or stunning scenes; it's about finding beauty in the ordinary, in the candid smiles, in the subtle interactions, and in the interplay of light and shadow in everyday life.

Passionate about photography and videography, I'm always interested in collaborations and working together to grow and enhance our skills.
Are you ready to capture memories together? Feel free to reach out — I look forward to our work!

About Me

Hi, I'm Alex! The person behind the lens.

I'm just a guy who likes capturing moments and emotions as life passes by. Armed with my Fujifilm X100V, I adore strolling through the city, keeping an eye out for captivating situations that unfold before me as I become a silent observer.

In addition to street photography, I love creating memories with my GoPro Hero11. Whether motorbiking in the alps, diving through a fish swarm or recording a time-lapse during sunrise, I always take it with me and it helps me to capture my story the way I lived it.
